Our Philosophy

Our Philosophy

Born from
embers & obsession

Cendere — from the Italian for “ash” — was founded on a single conviction: that pizza is not fast food. It is a living craft passed down through generations of Neapolitan pizzaioli, deserving the same reverence as any fine cuisine.

Our dough ferments for a minimum of 72 hours in temperature-controlled caves. Our tomatoes are hand-selected San Marzano DOP. Our fior di latte is flown in weekly from Agerola. Nothing here is an afterthought.
